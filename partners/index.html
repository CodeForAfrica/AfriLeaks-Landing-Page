Information about the temporary secondary receiver

Part of the fundamental design of afriLeaks is that submitted materials can only be accessed by those to whom they were addressed. This is enforced using strong encryption, which is a bit harder to use than normal email.

Until all of you have gained some experience with these tools, we have proposed the temporary model of a secondary receiver. It means that ANCIR technical advisor Friedrich Lindenberg will receive a copy of all submitted content.

We will not engage in any journalistic analysis of the material without your explicit say-so - that job is still yours, although ANCIR is happy to support your investigations through it's iLab.

The secondary receiver means that if you're having trouble with the platform, a copy of the material will exist that you can work with.
